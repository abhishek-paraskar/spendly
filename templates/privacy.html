Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. When you use Spendly to track your finances, we collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the core features of the Service — logging your expenses, generating spending summaries, and helping you understand your financial patterns. We do not sell your personal or financial data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal expense data with third-party advertisers. We may rely on trusted third-party providers for infrastructure needs such as hosting, which may process data on our behalf solely to operate the Service.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
